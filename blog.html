Essential Tax Deductions Every Small Business Should Know

Navigating small business tax deductions is essential for financial success. This guide explores key deductible expenses, from basic operating costs like rent and utilities to employee wages and professional services. For home-based businesses, portions of household expenses may be claimed, including mortgage interest and utilities. Digital expenses such as software and cloud services are also deductible. Proper record-keeping is crucial, requiring thorough documentation of receipts, statements, and asset purchases. Common pitfalls include mixing personal and business expenses and inadequate documentation. Professional guidance is recommended to maximize deductions while maintaining compliance with ever-changing tax regulations.
